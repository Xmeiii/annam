Pancake

Pancake is a pastry originating from poetic France, the cake is made from egg white, powdered sugar, granulated sugar, almond flour and added food coloring. The inside of the macaron filling will often be filled with jam, ganache (melted chocolate sauce), buttercream ... sandwich evenly between the two sides of the cake.

Banana Split

Banana Split is three ice cream balls in a long plate, flanked by 2 halves of a banana. The three ice creams are usually strawberry ice cream topped with chocolate syrup, chocolate ice cream with pineapple, vanilla ice cream topped with strawberry syrup. Each capsule is garnished with whipped cream and berry red cherries.
